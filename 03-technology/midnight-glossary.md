# Midnight Glossary

Reference glossary for key concepts related to the Midnight Network.

This document collects important terms used in the Midnight ecosystem.

## Midnight

A privacy-focused blockchain platform designed to enable confidential smart contracts and selective disclosure of data.

## Compact

The programming language used to develop smart contracts on the Midnight Network.

Compact is designed to support privacy-aware computation and selective disclosure.

## NIGHT

The primary ecosystem token associated with the Midnight Network.

Potential roles include:

- ecosystem incentives
- governance participation
- alignment of builders and community

## DUST

A network resource accounting unit used within the Midnight system.

DUST may represent the computational resources required to execute transactions or smart contracts.

## Selective Disclosure

A privacy mechanism that allows users to reveal only specific pieces of information when necessary.

This concept is central to Midnight's privacy model.

## Zero-Knowledge Proofs

Cryptographic methods that allow a party to prove the validity of a statement without revealing the underlying information.

These techniques are often used in privacy-preserving blockchain systems.

## Nightforce

A community initiative supporting the growth of the Midnight ecosystem.

Participants contribute through:

- research
- developer experimentation
- community engagement

## Midnight Ecosystem

The broader community of developers, researchers, builders, and users exploring the Midnight Network and its applications.
